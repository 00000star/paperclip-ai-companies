Operating model

What every Paperclip company needs

The point is not “AI agents”. The point is a repeatable commercial machine with clear boundaries.

Wedge offer

A narrow service that can be sold this week and delivered from a checklist.

Agent org

Roles with ownership: sales, ops, builder, QA, growth, and executive decision-making.

Skills

Reusable operating procedures that let agents produce consistent outputs.

QA gates

Stop bad handoffs before they reach a client or public user.

Unit economics

Entry, core, premium, and retainer pricing with delivery effort assumptions.

Risk limits

Explicit claims the company must not make and work it should reject or defer.
